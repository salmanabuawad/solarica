### 1 — Vision
**The trusted execution layer for renewable energy delivery.**
- Solarica turns engineering drawings into a live, verified execution model — the Execution Twin.
- *The single source of truth that solar construction has never had.*

### 2 — Problem
**Solar projects are built on broken information flow.**
- Drawings → PDF → Excel → WhatsApp → meetings → manual reports → decisions.
- Tens of thousands of components, hundreds of subcontractors, 12–24 months.
- Every hand‑off loses fidelity → disputes, rework, risk.

### 3 — No Single Source of Truth
**Three answers to "what's done?" — none of them trusted.**
- Engineering, field tracker, and payment sheet all disagree.
- No system can independently state what is installed, verified, and safe.
- Decisions are made in meetings, on opinion.

### 4 — Execution Twin
**A live model of reality, measured against engineering intent.**
- Every block, row, tracker, pier, string, panel, device — structured.
- Continuously reconciled with field progress, tests, materials, defects.
- Intent (BIM/drawings) describes the plan; the Execution Twin describes reality.

### 5 — Market Pain (universal & recurring)
**Every project, every year, has the same six failures.**
- Progress disputes · payment disputes · silent revisions · QA gaps · blockers · no source of truth.
- Not project‑specific — structural to the industry.

### 6 — Solution
**Solarica: Execution Intelligence Platform.**
- EPL ingests the drawing package → exact execution model.
- Field verification, payment, test, safety, change & blocker intelligence on one model.
- Offline‑native for real sites.

### 7 — EPL (Execution Preparation Layer)  ← built & proven
**Drawings → execution model, automatically and exactly.**
- Vector CAD/PDF parsing (no rasterization); full layer/semantic extraction.
- Physical rows, trackers, piers, strings, panels, devices.
- *This is the hard, defensible core — and it works today.*

### 8 — Verified Progress
**% complete that is measured, not claimed.**
- Definition‑of‑Done per component + evidence capture (photo/test/scan).
- Independent, auditable completion — the basis for trust.

### 9 — Payment Intelligence
**Pay for what is verified, not what is claimed.**
- Contractor claims matched to verified progress.
- Approval workflow + payment eligibility → fewer disputes, less over‑billing.

### 10 — Blocker Intelligence
**Model the blockers, not just chat about them.**
- Access, material, dependency blockers tracked on the model.
- Quantified schedule impact.

### 11 — Safety & Structural Intelligence
**Catch missing fasteners and structural defects at scale.**
- Missing‑bolt / structural‑defect detection + escalation.
- Safety = structural + financial + schedule + liability risk, made visible.

### 12 — Change Intelligence
**Revisions stop being silent.**
- Drawing revision → automatic impact on built & claimed scope.
- Rework caught in hours, not weeks.

### 13 — CAD Intelligence
**The drawing is the database.**
- Layer‑level understanding (e.g., color‑coded strings, structural layers).
- Deterministic, BOM‑exact extraction; improves with every project ingested.

### 14 — BHK Case Study
**From a raw package to an exact model — automatically.**
- 107 physical rows + tracker/pier geometry reconstructed.
- **24,130 piers extracted matching BOM exactly.**
- **288 strings detected at 100%** — 44 panels each, fully numbered, cross‑row resolved.
